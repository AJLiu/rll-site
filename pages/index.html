The Georgia Tech Robot Learning Lab(GTRLL) performs fundamental and applied research in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and robotics with a focus on developing theory and systems that tightly integrate perception, learning, and control. Our work touches on a range of problems including computer vision, system identification, forecasting, simultaneous localization and mapping, imitation learning, motion planning, and optimal control. The algorithms that we develop often use and extend theory from matrix and tensor algebra, nonparametric statistics, deep neural networks, non-convex optimization, online learning, and dynamic programming.
